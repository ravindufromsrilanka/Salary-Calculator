Salary Terms & Deduction Policy

1. General Terms & Conditions

  • • Agreed Salary Structure: The total agreed salary is composed of Basic Salary (40%), Fixed Allowances (45%), and Attendance Incentives (15%).
  • • EPF/ETF Contributions: Statutory contributions (EPF 8%/12% and ETF 3%) are calculated strictly based on the 40% Basic Salary to ensure long-term financial security.
  • • Paid Leave Policy: Employees are entitled to a maximum of 2 approved paid leaves per month. Any absence beyond this limit is categorized as "No-Pay."

2. Deduction Calculation Logic

Attendance Bonus (10%) - "All or Nothing"

This is a reward for perfect attendance. Recording even one (1) "No-Pay" day results in the total forfeiture of this 10% component.

Attendance Allowance (5%) - "Pro-Rata"

Deducted proportionally based on the number of No-Pay days. Formula: (Total Allowance / 25 Working Days) × Number of No-Pay Days.

Basic & Fixed Components

The Basic Salary (40%) and Fixed Allowances (45%) remain protected and are not subject to attendance-based penalties.
