Salary Calculation Terms & Conditions

1. Earnings Structure:

  • Basic Salary: Fixed at 40% of the Agreed Salary (Used for EPF/ETF calculations).
  • Fixed Allowances: 45% of Agreed Salary is distributed across Transport, Housing, Medical, COL, and Technical allowances.
  • Performance Incentives: Includes Attendance Allowance (5%) and Attendance Bonus (10%).

2. Deduction Policy:

  • Basic Protection: No deductions are ever made from the Basic Salary (40%).
  • Leave Entitlement: Up to 2 leaves (Casual/Medical) per month are fully paid and carry no deductions.
  • No-Pay Deduction: Any leave beyond the entitlement or without medical proof is deducted solely from the 15% Attendance Incentives.
  • Daily Deduction Rate: Calculated as (Total Attendance Pay / 25) per No-Pay day.
