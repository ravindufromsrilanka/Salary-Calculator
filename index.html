Salary Terms & Deduction Policy

1. Attendance Bonus (10%):

Awarded for perfect attendance. Any recorded 'No-Pay' day (beyond the 2-day paid leave limit) results in a total forfeiture (100% loss) of this bonus component.

2. Attendance Allowance (5%):

This component is pro-rated. Each 'No-Pay' day results in a deduction calculated as (Total Allowance / 25 working days).

3. Basic Salary (40%):

The Basic Salary is protected and never subjected to attendance-based deductions, ensuring consistency in EPF/ETF contributions.

4. Fixed Allowances:

Allowances for Transport (15%), Housing (10%), Medical (10%), COL (5%), and Tech (5%) are fixed and not affected by minor attendance shifts.
